About ZYPWN

The Zimbabwe Young Professional Women's Network (ZYPWN) is a dynamic platform dedicated to empowering young professional women through mentorship, capacity-building, and authentic engagement. Our foundation is built on the vision of creating a safe, inspiring space for women to connect, learn, and grow.

Meet the Team

Telma Nyanhanda

Founder

Telma Nyanhanda is the visionary Founder and Convener of the Zimbabwe Young Professional Women's Network (ZYPWN). She is a dedicated finance professional and development practitioner deeply passionate about women's empowerment, financial education, and ethical leadership.

Tendai Kachidza

Adminstrator

Tendai Kachidza is ZYPWN's Network Administrator, she is vital for strategic planning, budget management, and coordinating all network initiatives. Leveraging her expertise as a Solutions Architect, she ensures the digital platforms and systems are efficient, structured, and ready to support the entire member experience.
