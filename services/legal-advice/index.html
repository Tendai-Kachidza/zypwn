Summary

Secure your professional future with legal clarity. Our Legal Confidence Circle Service provides ZYPWN members with essential guidance, workshops, and access to in-house legal professionals who specialize in the areas most critical to young women in business and the workplace. We equip you with the knowledge to negotiate confidently, protect your interests, and mitigate professional risk.


What You Get

  • Direct Access to Legal Expertise — Get clear insights from our in-house lawyers on labor issues, professional contracts, and risk management.

  • Specialized Legal Focus — We focus on key areas that impact your growth, including corporate law (for entrepreneurs) and commercial law (for contract review and business transactions).

  • Know-Your-Rights Workshops — Interactive sessions providing essential knowledge on workplace discrimination, ethical employment practices, and navigating legal disputes.

  • Actionable Legal Frameworks — Clear checklists, template guidance, and data you can use for due diligence, securing agreements, and making sound professional decisions.


Why It Matters

Legal ambiguity is a career hazard. The confidence to excel in your profession requires the competence to protect it. Our service ensures you are not navigating contracts, workplace politics, or business deals alone. We empower you to act decisively, negotiate favorable terms, and build a stable professional future, just as we helped members achieve property ownership through favorable legal negotiation.


How It Works

  1. Issue Identification — Submit a query detailing the type of guidance you need (e.g., contract review, labor law question, corporate startup issue).

  2. Specialist Assignment — We match your query with the appropriate in-house legal specialist (Corporate or Commercial Law).

  3. Guided Consultation — You receive clear, general guidance and resources to help you understand your legal position and next steps.

  4. Community Learning — You gain automatic access to our Legal Confidence Circle forums and workshops for ongoing capacity building.


Optional Add-ons

  • One-on-One Contract Negotiation Coaching

  • Financial Management and Digital Literacy Integration Workshops (in collaboration with the Founder's expertise)

  • Access to exclusive Legal & Finance Masterclasses

  • Guidance on Property Investment Documentation (leveraging our past experience)
